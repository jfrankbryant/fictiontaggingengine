Help me Find the Fic!

This is an Umbrella Academy fit that I've been trying to find for days now. I can't remember where I saw it or any specific phrase but in it, Diego and Klaus are captured and held by at least two people. They decide to kill Klaus, but none of them know he will come back. Once they realize he won't stay dead after many attempts at killing him, they decide to just keep finding different ways to do so. After a while of it, Diego is pretty much disassociated, but Klaus manages to get out of his bonds and go to him. Diego kinda doesn't believe he's alive. Another thing, every time Klaus dies, the little girl in the sky becomes more sympathetic, but keeps sending him back anyway. I think the people that are holding them wanted information, maybe from Diego, could have been both.
