There's a small viewport in one of the doors, and we all crowd around it to watch the planet fall away beneath us, a twinkling blue sphere that rapidly looses definition as the ship accelerates.  We pass one of the moons, although it's pull is barely detectable in space, and then we're out into the solar system and rapidly leaving the place behind.

I feel a tug on my hand a minute later.

"Hey,"  It's Vincent.

"...Is for horses," I reply, turning around and freezing in place when I see him actually grinning at me.  "Uh..."

He holds up his free hand, and my brain kinda stops working.

Because he's holding three things in that hand:

Two brushes.

And a tube of toothpaste.
