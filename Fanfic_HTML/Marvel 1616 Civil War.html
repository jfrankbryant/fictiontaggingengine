Summary

My adaptation of the event comic Civil War, set in the continuity of Marvel 1616, although a few parts will be changed to better fit with the rest of this universe.

Marvel 1616: Civil War

We begin in The White House. Dell Rusk, the current President Of The United States, is watching a Daily Bugle news report concerning the latest events involving The Avengers.

Jameson: Earth's Mightiest Heroes have suffered a great loss. Founding Avenger Janet Pym was killed in action by longtime adversary, Ultron. Every hero is mourning for her loss. Mainstay Avengers Iron Man and Ant-Man have resigned due to their close relationship with the fallen hero, and Hawkeye retired to spent more time with his family. And now the weather with-.

Rusk has the TV switched off as he sits in silence. A grin forms on his face until Secretary Of Defence Maria Hill enters his office.

Rusk: Ah, Ms. Hill.

Maria Hill: Mr. President, you wanted to see me?

Rusk: Yes, I just want your opinion on something. What are your thoughts on The Avengers?

Maria Hill: (really long sigh) Sir, they are the best of the best but some fear that they have too much power. More importantly, they answer to no one.

Rusk: You believe they will make a mistake?

Maria doesn't answer after five seconds.

Maria Hill: Yes. Once that happens, we need to have a plan to prevent future mistakes.

Rusk: What do you suggest we do?

Maria Hill: Register with the government.

Rusk: I see. (beat) That's fine, Ms. Secretary.

Hill leaves the office. When she does, Rusk calls someone on his phone.

Rusk: Hello. Just a friend. Have you ever wanted to fight The Avengers? Good. Why? Because I will pay you for a special job in Stamford.

The scene cuts to black as Rusk hangs up the phone.
