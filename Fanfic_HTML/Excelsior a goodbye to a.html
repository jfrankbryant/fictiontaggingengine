Excelsior: a goodbye to a creator of Heroes

Today November 12, 2018 we lost someone central to fandom itself. Stan Lee has passed away at the age of 95. He has created so many iconic characters from Spider-Man to Black Panther among many other characters we will cherish until the end of time. It is hard to put into words what I can say about Stan. He was a creative genius in every sense of the word. Whenever I went to see a Marvel movie, I would always be on the look out for him to make a cameo and whenever he was on screen it was always fun to see him interact with many of the characters he has created over the course of 50 plus years. I am running out of things to say about Stan, I mean it is hard to put into words how big of an impact he has left on us as fans and how his characters resonated with us. I will always regret never getting a chance to meet him. 

Goodbye Stan, we love you and I hope your wife is waiting for you wherever you are.
